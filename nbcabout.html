About Us

Blue Ice
Hunsett Mill
Blue Ice Stern

Blue Ice is a 26 foot luxury sports cruiser which is licensed to carry up to six passengers. It has a large comfortable upper deck area which can be covered or open depending in weather conditions. The galley area below deck comes equipped with toilet facilities and further seating. We have a fridge on board and would encourage you to bring your own picnic and drinks, although we can provide food and drinks from local suppliers at your request.

We are based at Barton Broad, which is one of the largest Broads in the North Norfolk area. We can collect and drop off passengers from a number of locations to suit your specific needs with easy access on and off the boat. We are able to cruise in any weather and have a full canopy to protect passengers from inclement weather if necessary!
